About Us

Western Trading Company is a 27-year-old firm established in 1995. The company has been at the forefront of bringing the latest and most innovative technologies to the state of Gujarat.

Western Trading Company was formed with a vision “To improve patients’ quality of life in India by providing the best surgical technology”.

Currently, we are Channel Partners of Global Medical Device companies like Medtronic. Bbraun, Spatz and Rocket.

Our History

Western Trading Company is a proprietary business of Mr. Jaimin Joshi. Our proprietor studied Master’s in Geology. He
1990: He started his career with Sales Job at Raptakos & Brett based in Vadodara.
1991: After learning and excelling at his sales job, he was offered an opportunity to work with Johnson & Johnson in the suture business.
1992: With his excellent performance, he was selected for Ethicon Indo-Surgery. During those times laparoscopy was a new radical approach to Lap-cholecystectomy and to spread
awareness of this new modality he was trained in Anatomy and Surgical skills at Cincinnati Training Centre of Ethicon Indo-Surgery.
1995: After training close to 150 surgeons and running around a lot he decided to start his own distribution network. Rest is History.
